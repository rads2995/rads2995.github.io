About Me

My name is Rodrigo Duran. I am a mechanical engineer with a passion for scientific computing, robotics, control theory, machine learning, and aritificial intelligence.
My research interests and experience include wearable exoskeletons, data-driven nonlinear modeling, and convex optimization. My goal is to work as a Guidance, Navigation, and Control (GNC) engineer with a focus on algorithm design and development, modeling and simulation, and nonlinear autonomous systems.
